games

This is a gallery of experimental indie games I've either made or helped make. These were developed during special events called "game jams", which usually take place over a single weekend. Some of these (such as the Ludum Dare games) I've developed myself (which means I created all of the assets such as music and graphics within the alloted time), while others I've helped create as part of a team (as is the case for Global Game Jam games). In any case, they're all free to be played either online or on some other platform, and their code is available if you're interesting in finding out how they were made. Enjoy!


  • Jan 2013 » Twisted Shotgun Panic Cave: A Global Game Jam 2013 Entry (Google Play)
    An arcade highscore-based game, in the style of Super Crate Box. Created in 48 hours as a Global Game Jam 2013 entry.


  • Dec 2012 » Lunar Frenzy: A Ludum Dare 25 Entry
    Find out for how long you can survive as a rage-fueled moon making its way through the universe, while shaking off explosive mines!


  • Apr 2012 » Disregard All Humans: A Ludum Dare 23 Entry
    Help the astro-dinosaur destroy every city in its path, eat their inhabitants, and grow to consume the planet!


  • Jan 2012 » Flamey: A Global Game Jam 2012 Entry (Play on your browser!)
    A platformer with a twist! Play through the same level over and over to fetch the fire orbs, but watch out! As shadows of your former self return to haunt you!
