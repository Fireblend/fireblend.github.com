about me

My name is Sergio. I'm a Costa Rican programmer currently working as a mobile software engineer at Cecropia Solutions, and a computer science M.Sc. student at ITCR. I enjoy making games for fun, and aim to release a successful indie game someday. Meanwhile, I participate at game jams and experiment with new technology and concepts as often as I can.

Among other things, I'm a sci-fi fan, a free/open software and hardware enthusiast, a photography novice, an avid reader, a traveler, a skeptic and a humanist. If not coding and listening to music, it's just the music.
