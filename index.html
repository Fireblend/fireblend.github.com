about me

My name is Sergio, and I live in Costa Rica. I currently split my time between working as a mobile software engineer at Growth Acceleration Partners, giving lectures for introductory CS courses at ITCR, and studying for my M.Sc. degree at the same university. I enjoy making games for fun and toying around with new tech & tools when time allows.

Among other things, I'm a sci-fi fan, a free/open software and hardware enthusiast, a photography novice, an avid reader, a traveler and a humanist. When not coding and listening to music, it's just the music.
