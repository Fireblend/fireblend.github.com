about me

My name is Sergio. I'm a Costa Rican programmer currently working as a mobile software engineer at Cecropia Solutions, and a computer science M.Sc. student at ITCR. I enjoy making games for fun, participating at game jams and experimenting with new technology and tools as often as I can.

Among other things, I'm a sci-fi fan, a free/open software and hardware enthusiast, a photography novice, an avid reader, a traveler and a humanist. When not coding and listening to music, it's just the music.
